Work Experience

2022 - 2023

İkartu Dinamik

Intern

Ikartu is a Research and Development company specializing in virtual reality and the metaverse. Additionally, they have previously undertaken simulation projects. This is why they occasionally utilize microcontrollers to simulate real-life scenarios, such as ESP boards. During our initial meeting, they inquired about my interests, to which I expressed a desire to work with microcontrollers. In response, they recommended that I learn about ESP boards. Not only are they cost-effective, but they also offer faster performance compared to most other microcontrollers. Furthermore, they come equipped with an integrated onboard WI-FI + Bluetooth combo, making them even more versatile.

  • After these initial discussions, I was assigned a project to learn about the ESP 12F board. The task involved connecting the ESP 12F module with an MPU6050 gyro + accelerometer sensor and transmitting the collected data to a Unity simulation through UDP communication. As a starting point, I designed a PCB board as requested, which turned out to be visually impressive. However, I couldn't test it because it was only a prototype that needed to be purchased from China. Instead, I decided to build the circuit on a breadboard. Surprisingly, everything worked perfectly. The board successfully connected to my Wi-Fi, read the values from the MPU6050 sensor, and transmitted them to the Unity program I had developed. Ultimately, the project was successfully completed.
  • Through this project, I gained valuable experience in utilizing ESP boards, and I believe it has been a significant learning opportunity for me. ESP boards hold great importance due to their affordability, speed, compact size, utility, and user-friendly nature. They are widely employed in various Internet of Things (IoT) projects. This experience has instilled confidence in my ability to undertake similar endeavors in the future.
